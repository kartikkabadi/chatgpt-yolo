# Code of conduct

YOLO’s community should be practical, respectful, and welcoming.

- Discuss ideas and code, not personal characteristics.
- Give specific, actionable feedback.
- Assume good intent while remaining rigorous about safety and correctness.
- Do not harass, threaten, discriminate against, or expose private information about others.
- Respect maintainers’ decisions on scope, safety, and release quality.

Unacceptable behavior may be removed, and contributors may be restricted from participating. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer rather than escalating them in public threads.

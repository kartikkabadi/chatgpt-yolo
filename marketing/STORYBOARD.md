## Copy rules

- No "AI agent", "autonomous", "fully autonomous", "unlimited", or OpenAI-endorsement implications.
- Slash actions are shown as **YOLO commands**, not native ChatGPT commands.
- The workflow bound is visible up front and ends/pauses — never an endless loop.
- GitHub is connected to ChatGPT, not to YOLO.
- Every full sentence dwells ≥ 1.8 s; short chips ≥ 1.2 s; the boundary label and legal line dwell ≥ 3 s.

## Square adaptation

Recompose, do not blind crop. Stack the headline above the product surface, keep the 4-segment progress bound and the GitHub boundary label at full size, and verify no clipped square layout.
